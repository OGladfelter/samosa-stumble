SAMOSA STUMBLE IV

Rules

  • Eat a samosa...
  • Stumble through a 1.1 mile loop...
  • ...in 20 minutes
  • Then repeat until you quit!
  • The timer re-starts every 20 minutes, at the :00, :20, and :40 of each hour
  • You must line up at starting line to eat a samosa on these times
  • If you're late or refuse to eat another samosa, you're out! Samosa wins!

The last samosa stumbler standing wins! But only after eating one more samosa and running a solo loop.

Event Info

  • Location: Warren Park, Chicago
  • Meet: the start & finish are inside the park, just east of Warren Park Ball Field 5
  • Round 1 starts at 5:00pm
  • Course: 1.1 mile loop around the park
  • We'll have water and samosas at the start / finish. Bring whatever else you want...but what beyond samosas do you really need?
